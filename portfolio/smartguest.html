Design App Interface

SmartGuest started as an idea to improve experiences for guests in hotels. When I started to contribute by March 2020, the visual identity was already created, so I started designing interfaces and suggesting new ideas for user flows.

My role: I'm mainly responsible for creating the app interface as well as defining user flows and presenting lo-fi and hi-fi app design.

Team: Me & LA Design

Duration: 4 months (March 2020 - Current)

About SmartGuest

SmartGuest is an app created and developed by friends to facilitate check-ins and check-outs in hotels and also to improve the guest's experiences overall.

The process

Everyone involved had a clear idea of what the app should do: facilitate guest's check-ins and check-outs in hotels. The first screens defined to be designed for this app were Login, Reservations, Statement and Profile.

Icons & Graphics

One of the things that I like to work on when designing user interfaces is create unique icons and illustrations to reinforce the brand's identity.

I created icons to complement information, make it more understandable and easy to read.

Boleto Simples also needed to explain how they connect to other banks and process payment slip between the bank and other companies. So I illustrated the process.
