Designing for Semco Style & LeadWise

I was responsible for designing and developing the visual identity of LeadWise and later the Semco Style Toolkit project.

Based on LeadWise's values and goals, I designed a clean and modern visual focusing on their content.

About LeadWise

LeadWise was created to help leaders to build the best workplaces where people are proud of by offering online courses and tools through an international network of changemakers.

Icons

One of the things that I like to work on when designing user interfaces is create unique icons and illustrations to reinforce the brand's identity.

I created icons to complement information, make it more understandable and easy to read.

Semco Style Toolkit

Through LeadWise I was able to work on some projects for Semco Style Institute as well, such as the Semco Style Toolkit.

It's where all the exercises and workshop classe's contents are loaded. It was built in WordPress, has a login area, translation and it's an easy platform to update content.
