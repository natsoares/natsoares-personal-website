Redesigning for Boleto Simples

I was responsible for the redesign of the Boleto Simples website. The objective was to create a new visual identity to represent it as a modern, trustful and stable financial company.

About Boleto Simples

Boleto Simples helps other companies, such as e-commerces, to generate a brazilian payment method called "boleto", a payment slip, a paper for debt payments.

This paper can only be generated by a bank so Boleto Simples intermediate this process in an easy and effective way between the bank and other companies.

Typography

Boleto Simples was using a Serif font on it's heading that was not connected to any other typography or element of the page. In order to reinforce the brand's identity, bring a more modern, light and yet solid message, I changed all title's typography to "Quicksand" that matches closer to the brand.

Icons & Graphics

One of the things that I like to work on when designing user interfaces is create unique icons and illustrations to reinforce the brand's identity.

I created icons to complement information, make it more understandable and easy to read.

Boleto Simples also needed to explain how they connect to other banks and process payment slip between the bank and other companies. So I illustrated the process.
