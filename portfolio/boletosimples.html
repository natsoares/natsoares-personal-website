Boleto Simples

Boleto Simples is a company that helps other companies, such as e-commerces, to generate a Brazilian payment method called "Boleto". This payment is generated by a bank and Boleto Simples intermedeate this process in an easy way between the bank and other companies.

I was responsible to redesigned Boleto Simples website. There was a need to create new visual presentations for their clients, not only show their identity but also to represent how stable they are.

I'm now in a process of redesigning all their internal pages as well as their internal communication.
